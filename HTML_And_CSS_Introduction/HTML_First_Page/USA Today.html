The cable News Network is a multinational news based pay any television channel headquartered in Atlanta, United States. It is a part of of AT&T's WarnerMedia. It was founded in 1980 by American media proprietor Ted Turner and Resse Schonfeld as a 24-hour cable news channel.

Upon its launch in 1980, CNN was the first television channel to provide 24-hour news coverage, and was the first all-news television channel in the United States.

Globally, CNN programming has aired through CNN International, seen by viewers in over 212 countries and territories.
